About

Code Veda 3.0, the signature event of department of Computer Science and Engineering of Mangalam College of Engineering is an annual technical fiesta. This is the best platform to present your talents in technical domain at its best. It consists of a melange of competitive events, Workshops on latest technologies and entertainment oriented events, there by catering to a multitude of students from various domains.
